✅Why 2× Per Week Works

Ben Patrick confirmed in 2023–24 that 2–3 sessions per week was his own “secret weapon” while time-constrained. Tendons adapt slower than muscle — 48+ hours between sessions produces better structural adaptation. For beginners and maintenance, 2× is not just acceptable; it’s often optimal.

📋 The 9-Step Routine
🔥
BURN = BUILD
Muscular burn = progress. No burn = stand farther from wall.
🚫
PAIN = STOP
Joint pain = regress. Never push through it.
⚠️ Precautions
  • • Sharp joint pain → stop & reduce range of motion immediately
  • • Post-surgery or acute injury → consult physio before starting
  • • Tibialis raises: stand closer to wall to reduce difficulty
  • • ATG Split Squat: elevate front foot if hip flexors are tight
  • • Couch Stretch: skip if patellar tendon is acutely inflamed
  • • Always brisk-walk 2 min before beginning • Flat shoes or bare feet
